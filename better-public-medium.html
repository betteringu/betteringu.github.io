Better Public Medium

Aims to become the Better Public Medium for accessing media and interacting with other beings.

There will be an unlimited number of ways of interacting with things and beings on the ING.

There will be many different “flavors” of BPM to choose from, from theoretically any source, as otherwise this would be a boring monopoly that everyone is upset with.

Iron sharpens iron and competition is the best way for BPM to get better.

Since the EVERY-ING aims to encapsulate literally every-ing, all of the posts on all other platforms can be indexed by the ING and thus will be available via BPM.

Like Youtube or Spotify, but better because you can access every video or song on the ING.

Like Facebook, Instagram, or X, but better because it is platform agnostic and recursively encapsulates all of the other platforms.
