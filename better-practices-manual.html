Better Practices Manual

“Best Practices” is a commonly used term in the world of Engineering.

Better Practices are like Best Practices, only Better.

This BPM aims to create an InterNetGraph mapping what all of the best engineers think are the “Better Practices” for doing any particular kind of thing.

Naturally, over time we will make it better.

An important piece of the puzzle in making this work is in developing sufficient mechanisms for coming to a General General Consensus about any particular thing or ing.
