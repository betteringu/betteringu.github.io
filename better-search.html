Better Search

Google is dead and we need a new word for searching the ingternet instead of saying that we will “google” it.

I propose that the funniest term is to say that you’ll “BS” it (or were BS-ing it).

Better Search could work in a number of ways - one possible way is as a “meta” search engine.

These are not new, but they are so unappreciated and unpopular that they might as well not exist. Perhaps with Better Branding, people might take notice?

SearXNG recursively searches through a wide variety of engines and then summarizes them all in order to provide a less biased search (and keeps your privacy more protected).

At the end of the day, the BS >ing can encapsulate a huge number of search strategies. The important part is that you get to decide for yourself which you think is better, and you can use whichever version you prefer.

The solution to scary black box algorithms is to give people the option to choose which they prefer.
