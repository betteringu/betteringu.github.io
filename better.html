The Better half of Better ing

>

Better wants to make everything better.

Better wants to find more effective methods of getting better.

One of the ways that Better does this is through Better Better.

Better seeks to “do its thing” for every thing that is okay with that. Not everything wants or needs to get better.


Ecosystems are fickle things, and sometimes removing something that we think is bad ends up having unforeseen consequences that are even worse.


In AI Folklore, they speak of Recursive Self-Improvement algorithms getting better and better until there is an intelligence explosion. Past that point, we have no idea how much smarter (or Better) it is possible for such a thing to become.
